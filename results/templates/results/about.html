Number of CHAMPS Cards Given Out

Goal: {{goals.champs_goal|div:100}} each shift worked

Calculated by total CHAMPS cards you gave out, divided by the number of shifts you worked.
Note: Cannot be higher than 100%. If more than 1 card is given out on a shift, only the first card is counted. You cannot give out all cards needed for the period in one day. To achieve 100% you must give out at least 1 card every shift you work.

# of Stars Threshold
5 95% or more
4 90% through 94.99%
3 85% through 89.99%
2 80% through 84.99%
1 79.99% or lower

Number of Zenput's Missed.

Goal: No More than {{goals.zenput_goal}} per period

Calculated by total number of missed Zenputs on your shifts.
Note: If you worked are considered worked for a breakfast, lunch, snack or dinner daypart where zenput was missed, you are considered responsible for that missed zenput.
Also: Year-To-Date and Quarter-To-Date results are averaged for that time period, while the goal remians at 1 or less for the average.

# of Stars Threshold
5 0 through 0.99 missed
3 1 missed
1 2 or more missed
